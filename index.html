WELCOME TO GREEDFEST

The fourth birthday of PAYDAY has come and gone and with it came the introduction of the Black Market Update, introducing paid, stat-boosting microtransactions to PAYDAY 2. The PAYDAY community has rallied against these additions, and this page hopes to outline and keep track of community goals.
To help us send the message to OVERKILL that we don't want microtransactions in PAYDAY 2, consider leaving a review on the Steam page or Metacritic page. If you have any web development knowledge you can contribute to this site. To learn more, visit our github or join in on the discussion on our subreddit.
